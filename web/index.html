♠ Blackjack Counting Trainer

📖 How to Play

Cards appear on screen one by one, simulating a real blackjack shoe.


Use the Hi-Lo system to track the running count as each card is revealed:

  • 2 – 6  → +1  (low cards benefit the dealer)
  • 7 – 9  →  0  (neutral)
  • 10 / J / Q / K / A  → −1  (high cards benefit the player)

After the last card, type your running count and press Enter or click Confirm.


💡 In Practice Mode a small badge above each card shows its Hi-Lo value, so you can learn the system at your own pace.
🏆 Scoring

Points = 100 base + (level × 10) + (streak × 5, max 100) + speed bonus

Advance a level after 5 correct answers in a row.
